Boba Bean Bar

where every sip comes with a story. Our cafe is more than just a place to grab a drink. It's a haven for enthusiasts of premium coffee and bubble tea. Our signature Taiwanese bubble tea, with its delightful blend of tea, milk or fruit flavors, and the unique texture of tapioca pearls and popping boba, promises an unforgettable experience.

About Us

Behind Boba & Bean Bar are Divya and Vijay, a dynamic duo with over a decade of experience in the food and beverage sector. Their journey began with a shared passion for creating exceptional culinary experiences. Combining Divya’s expertise in management technology with Vijay’s innovative approach to menu creation and operational efficiency, they embarked on the Boba & Bean Bar adventure. United by a vision to offer a unique cafe experience, they are committed to bringing you the highest quality beverages and light meals in a cozy, welcoming environment.
